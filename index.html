Why study at UNIFACISA?

Studying at Unifacisa is a  unique opportunity . We are an institution concerned not only with its theoretical training, but also with its transformative role in society and its technical and practical skills.

We are honored to belong to the select group of the  5% best rated Higher Education Institutions in Brazil , according to the MEC (Ministry of Education).

Scholarships and Discounts

RUDDER

The cheapest financing in Brazil with an interest rate of 0.0%*. Credit payment begins only 30 days after completion or cancellation/locking of the course.
know more

CredIES

Student credit that allows students to pay 50% of their monthly fees during enrollment and the other 50% after completing the course.
know more

Egress

Students who have already completed another degree at Unifacisa have their first registration free and discounts on monthly fees throughout the course, including re-enrollment.
know more

Health insurance

Students receive discounts on tuition fees depending on the course of choice.
